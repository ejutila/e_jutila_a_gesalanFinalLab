Brazil

Brazil face Switzerland in their second match of Group G at the World Cup. The five-time world champions won their opening match 2-0 against Serbia thanks to a brace from Tottenham Hotspur forward Richarlison.

Switzerland, meanwhile, beat Cameroon 1-0 in their opening game. Both sides will be looking to pick up all three points to progress to the knockouts.

Iran

After a brutal 6-2 loss in its opener against England, Iran bounced back to beat Wales, 2-0, scoring both goals in extra time. Iran has never made it past the group stage in a World Cup but is now in a position to do just that.

A win against the U.S. would be enough for the Iranians to advance. Even a draw might be enough. Iran would advance with a draw unless Wales defeated England.

Portugal

After a scoreless first half in its opening match against Ghana, Portugal scored a flurry of goals in a 15-minute span to win, 3-2. It now sits atop Group H and is on track to advance. It faces Uruguay next.

All eyes were on Portugal’s Cristiano Ronaldo as he made his debut in Qatar just two days after his public breakup with Manchester United, and he marked the occasion by making history.
